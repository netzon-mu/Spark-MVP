How SPARK works
SPARK automatically receives improvement ideas submitted by Netzon agents and staff via Microsoft Forms. Each idea is processed by a series of AI agents that structure the problem, estimate operational impact, score it for priority, and check for quality. All AI outputs are reviewed and approved by humans before any action is taken. No idea is acted upon without human oversight.
📝
1. Submit
Agents submit ideas via MS Forms in under 3 minutes
🤖
2. AI processes
8 AI agents enrich, score, and quality-check the idea automatically
👥
3. Human review
Innovation Council reviews and approves ideas before any action
Human oversight: SPARK is an AI-assisted tool, not an autonomous decision-maker. Every enrichment, score, and categorisation can be reviewed, overridden, or dismissed by a human reviewer. Quarantined ideas require explicit human release before scoring proceeds. All governance decisions are made by the Innovation Council, not by the AI.
